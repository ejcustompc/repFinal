Computer Case

This Open Frame chassis sets a new benchmark in groundbreaking open frame chassis design. With full support for liquid cooling – the Expert Build is built from the ground up to make even the most advanced PC customization and hassle free, making it a dream to build with. The open frame panel design is protected with a clear acrylic panel – behind which sits the chassis frame, with supports the latest PC hardware, AIO and DIY liquid cooling solutions. Specially designed dual GPU and PSU layouts, plus a 3-way stand placement (wall mountable, horizontal laying and vertical standing) allow users to position the chassis any orientation – and appreciate your artwork status worthy build.
